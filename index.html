Internet deve essere al servizio del "bene pubblico": il manifesto di Tim Berners-Lee

Scritto da The Booleaner
il 10/10/2020

1st Photo: John S. and James L. K***ht Foundation, by Scott Henrichsen
2nd Photo: Wikimedia, by Paul Clarke

To the suprise of no one, Tim Berners-Lee announced that the Internet should serve the people and not the corporations. What a suprise, right? The man who didn't sell the Internet to corporations thinks Internet should be a human right. And honestly? He's right. Net Neutrality is essential, and the fact that no one bats an eye at corporations looking to take it away so they can make more money is beyond concerning. What the fuck is wrong with y'all? Are you seriously so brainwashed by capitalist propaganda that you believe companies charging you a fuckton of money for something that should be a basic right because you need it to work let alone LIVE in this digital age a good thing?
